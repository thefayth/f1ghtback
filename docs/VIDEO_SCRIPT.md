# Public Video Script

Target duration: 2 minutes 30 seconds. Use blank walkthrough screens, captions, and no personal or case material.

## 0:00-0:18 - Why

"I am Faith Atwater-Cheltenham, a Black mother and disabled technologist. I built f1ghtback because court papers can make the next safe action nearly impossible to see."

## 0:18-0:38 - Boundary

"This app has no account, upload, or case database. Personal answers stay in browser memory, are never sent to GPT, and disappear when the tab closes unless the user downloads a packet."

## 0:38-1:02 - Router

"I choose California, Respond, and my timing. f1ghtback gives one bounded next action, a short checklist, questions for human help, and checked official sources. Cross-state uncertainty blocks form-specific guidance."

## 1:02-1:42 - Guided Preparation

"Now I continue to the California FL-320 response coach. It helps identify the papers, record timing for human review, match requested orders, preserve my exact words, prepare a facts scaffold, list exhibits without uploading them, and track filing and service questions. Utah has a separate family answer coach."

## 1:42-2:02 - AI Boundary

"GPT-5.6 can explain this selected step, but receives only the guide ID, step ID, detail level, and official source metadata. It never receives what I type. If AI is unavailable, the official-source explanation still works."

## 2:02-2:20 - Packet

"The browser creates a PDF and companion text packet marked Draft, Not Filed, Human Review Required. It includes exact answers, unresolved questions, source versions, and a filing and service tracker. Nothing files or sends automatically."

## 2:20-2:30 - Close

"Codex helped build and test the guides, privacy boundaries, source monitoring, and release. f1ghtback turns overload into a prepared conversation with a human still in control."

## Capture Order

1. Title card.
2. California, Respond, Today router selections.
3. Next action and official sources.
4. Blank California FL-320 guide picker and progress rail.
5. Requested-orders and exact-words steps without entering personal content.
6. Explain-step boundary and source badge.
7. Draft PDF/text download controls.
8. End card with app and repository URLs.
